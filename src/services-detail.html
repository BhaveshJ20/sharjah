Services

Statistical Data Request

This service allows customers to request the latest data, reports and statistical indicators of the Emirate of Sharjah in various sectors such as economic, social, demographic and environmental.

Service Code: DSCD-STAT-001

Application Steps

1

Complete the form

2

Submit request

3

Receive approval

Request Service Now

Description and Requirements

Customer Experience

I would like to receive statistical data on the Emirate of Sharjah.

I have inquired about the services of the Statistics and Community Development Department and the request procedure 

Prior to the initiation of my service request, I conducted a search for the statistical information I need, using the quick search tool. The step was optional.

I was encouraged to provide feedback on my service request

I have received a response by e-mail or through the channels approved by the department mentioned in the service request.

I searched for the required statistical data

I was informed about the steps to follow in filling out the request form 

I submitted the application through the approved channel for submission.

I received a notification on the website and an email informing me of my request type, request ID, status and the expected date of delivery of the requested service.

I was encouraged to provide feedback on my service request experience

I provided feedback about my service request experience with the department.

Targeted Segment

Government agencies, private institutions and individuals in the Emirate of Sharjah and the United Arab Emirates.

Service Charges

NA

Delivery Time

Within 3 working days (provided that requirements and procedures are completed on time) or by agreement between the two sides​

Documents Required

  1. No documents required if the data is not confidential
  2. An official letter from the requesting entity is required if the data is confidential, stating reasons for requesting the data.  (Soft copies of all document are accepted)

Terms and Conditions

  1. In case requirements and procedures are not fully completed, customer request will be return for correction. 
  2. Other entities may make use of the data available on the website and the mobile app and exchange it with other parties (taking into account the usage policy), provided that the data that has been marked as confidential is used for internal purposes only- excluding as well the entity’s consulting offices- and ensuring non-disclosure to the public in any way except after a consent in writing from the Department of Statistics and Community Development.

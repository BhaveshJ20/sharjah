Training

Request for a Specialized Statistical Training Course

This service allows the customer to request training and specialized courses in the statistical field that in turn help to build capacities and competencies of those working in govermental and semi-govermental institutions in the Emirates of Sharjah and in the United Arab Emirates.

Service Code: DSCD-STAT-005

Schedule

Application Steps

1

Complete the form / Create an account

2

Submit request

3

Receive approval

Description and Requirements

Customer Experience

I wolud like to apply for a training course in the statistical field.

I received an email confirming the receipt of the course request.

I was informed about the steps to follow in filing out the request form.

I was encouraged to provide my feedback on experience of applying for the course.

I have inquired about the services of DSCD and how to apply for a specialized statistical training course.

My request was replied to by an approval or an apology.

I sumitted the request through one of the service request channels.

I provided my feedback and observations on my experience in dealing with DSCD.

Targeted Segment

Government amd semi-geverment employees in the Emirate of Sharjah and the United Arab Emirates, and UAE nationals in general according to the subject of the training course.

Service Charges

Cost of hosting, advertising and other logistics services.

Delivery Time

The required approvals are provided within 2 working days (provided that all requirements and procedures are completed on time)

Documents Required

Copy of UAE ID card (front and back)

Terms and Conditions

  1. In case requirements and procedures are not fully completed, customer request will be return for correction. 
  2. Other entities may make use of the data available on the website and the mobile app and exchange it with other parties (taking into account the usage policy), provided that the data that has been marked as confidential is used for internal purposes only- excluding as well the entity’s consulting offices- and ensuring non-disclosure to the public in any way except after a consent in writing from the Department of Statistics and Community Development.
